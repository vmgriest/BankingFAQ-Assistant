# Bank — Customer Policy Handbook (Mock / Synthetic)

_This is a fictional policy document created for demo purposes only. No real bank, rates, or terms are represented._

## 1. Account Types & Eligibility
The Bank offers Basic, Premium, and Youth Savings accounts, as well as Free, Interest, and Student Checking
accounts. New accounts may be opened by anyone 18+ with valid government ID; minors may open a Youth Savings or
Student Checking account with a parent/guardian as joint owner. Joint accounts are permitted on all product types.

## 2. Interest Rates
All deposit account rates are variable and reviewed monthly against the Federal Funds Rate. Certificates of Deposit
(CDs) carry a fixed rate for the selected term. Interest on savings and checking accounts compounds daily and is
credited monthly.

## 3. Loan Eligibility
Loan products include personal loans, mortgages, and auto loans. Minimum credit score for standard approval is 620.
Applicants below this threshold may be offered secured or co-signed alternatives at a higher rate. Income and
debt-to-income ratio are evaluated for all loan types.

## 4. Fees
The Bank charges standard industry fees for overdrafts, out-of-network ATM use, wire transfers, and returned
checks. Fees may be waived by maintaining minimum balances or enrolling in eligible account tiers. A full fee
schedule is available at any branch or on the Fees page of our website.

## 5. Transactions & Transfers
Daily withdrawal and purchase limits apply to all debit accounts for security purposes and can be adjusted by
contacting support. Internal transfers are instant; external ACH transfers take 1-3 business days unless same-day
service is requested. Customers have 60 days from a transaction date to file a dispute.

## 6. Customer Support
For anything outside the scope of this FAQ — account-specific issues, fraud reports, or complex disputes — customers
should contact support at 1-800-555-0199 or support@bank.example, available 24/7.
